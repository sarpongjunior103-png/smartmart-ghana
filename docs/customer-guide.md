## 1. Account Creation

### How to Register

1. Navigate to `https://smartmartghana.com/register`
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Phone number (optional but recommended)
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your email for a verification code
5. Enter the verification code to activate your account

### Email Verification

- A 6-digit verification code is sent to your email
- The code expires in 24 hours
- You can resend the code if you didn't receive it
- You must verify your email to place orders

### Account Benefits

- Track orders in real time
- Save items to your wishlist
- Earn loyalty points on every purchase
- Participate in the referral program
- Leave product reviews
- Chat with vendors
- Save shipping addresses for faster checkout
- View order history and receipts

### Social Login

You can also sign up or log in using:
- Google account
- Facebook account

## 3. AI Assistant

### What is the AI Assistant?

The AI Assistant is a smart search tool that helps you find products using natural language. It understands typos, partial words, and conversational queries.

### Using the AI Assistant

1. Click the **AI Assistant** icon (sparkle) in the search bar
2. Type your query in natural language, e.g.:
   - "I need a phone under 2000 GHS"
   - "Show me red sneakers size 42"
   - "Best laptop for students"
   - "Affordable headphones with good bass"
3. The AI will suggest relevant products
4. Click on any suggestion to view the product

### Spell Correction

The search includes automatic spell correction:
- "fone" → "phone"
- "laptob" → "laptop"
- "snekers" → "sneakers"
- Case-insensitive: "FONE" and "fone" both correct to "phone"

### Search Tips

- Use specific keywords for better results
- Include brand names if you have a preference
- Mention price ranges for budget filtering
- Use descriptive words (color, size, material)
- The AI works with partial words and abbreviations

## 4. Cart & Checkout

### Adding to Cart

1. Browse to a product page
2. Select quantity (and variant if applicable)
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. A notification confirms the item was added
5. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Managing Your Cart

Access your cart at **Cart** (top navigation):

- View all items in your cart
- Update quantities
- Remove items
- See subtotal, shipping, and total
- Apply coupon codes (if available)
- Save items for later (moves to wishlist)

### Checkout Process

1. **Review Cart**: Confirm items and quantities
2. **Shipping Address**: Select a saved address or add a new one
3. **Shipping Method**: Choose standard or express delivery
4. **Payment Method**: Select your preferred payment option
5. **Order Review**: Final review of all details
6. **Place Order**: Confirm and complete the purchase

### Payment Methods

| Method | Description | Processing Time |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| MTN Mobile Money | Pay via MTN MoMo | Instant |
| Vodafone Cash | Pay via Vodafone Cash | Instant |
| AirtelTigo Money | Pay via AirtelTigo | Instant |
| Credit/Debit Card | Visa, Mastercard | Instant |
| Paystack | Card or bank transfer | Instant |
| Stripe | International cards | Instant |
| Flutterwave | Card or bank transfer | Instant |
| Hubtel | Mobile money or card | Instant |

### Order Confirmation

After placing an order:
- You receive an order confirmation email
- Your order appears in your dashboard
- The vendor is notified to process your order
- You can track the order status in real time

### Shipping Costs

- Shipping costs vary by location and vendor
- Some vendors offer free shipping above a threshold
- Express shipping costs more but arrives faster
- Shipping costs are shown at checkout before you pay

## 5. Multi-Country & Multi-Language

### Country Selection

- Select your country from the country selector (top navigation)
- Available countries: Ghana, Nigeria, Côte d'Ivoire, Kenya, South Africa
- Product availability may vary by country
- Prices are displayed in the local currency
- Shipping options depend on your selected country

### Currency

- Default currency: GHS (Ghanaian Cedi)
- Other supported currencies: NGN (Nigerian Naira), KES (Kenyan Shilling), ZAR (South African Rand), USD (US Dollar)
- Currency conversion is automatic based on current exchange rates
- You are charged in the vendor's local currency

### Language

- Supported languages: English, Twi, Hausa, French
- Select your preferred language from the language selector
- Product descriptions are shown in the available language
- The AI Assistant supports queries in all supported languages

## 6. Order Tracking

### Tracking Your Order

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Dashboard → Orders**
3. Click on any order to see detailed tracking

### Order Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Pending | Order placed, waiting for vendor confirmation |
| Confirmed | Vendor has accepted the order |
| Processing | Vendor is preparing your items |
| Shipped | Order has been dispatched with a carrier |
| Delivered | Order has been delivered to your address |
| Cancelled | Order was cancelled (by you or vendor) |
| Refunded | Payment was refunded |

### Shipping Notifications

You receive email notifications when:
- Your order is confirmed by the vendor
- Your order is shipped (includes tracking number)
- Your order is delivered
- There are any status changes

### Tracking Number

Once your order is shipped:
- The tracking number appears in your order details
- Click the tracking number to track on the carrier's website
- Supported carriers: Ghana Post, DHL, FedEx, Aramex

### Delivery Issues

If you experience delivery issues:
- Check the tracking number for updates
- Contact the vendor via live chat
- Open a support ticket if the issue is unresolved
- Report non-delivery within 7 days of expected delivery

## 8. Reviews

### Writing a Review

1. After your order is delivered, go to **Dashboard → Orders**
2. Find the delivered order and click **Leave a Review**
3. Rate the product (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review text
5. Upload photos (optional, up to 3 images)
6. Submit the review

### Review Guidelines

- Be honest and factual
- Focus on the product, not the vendor
- Include pros and cons
- Mention specific features you liked or disliked
- Don't use offensive language
- Don't include personal information
- Reviews are moderated and may take 24 hours to appear

### Review Helpfulness

- Other customers can mark your review as helpful
- Helpful reviews appear higher in the list
- You can edit your review within 30 days of posting

## 9. Loyalty Program

### How It Works

- Earn points on every purchase (1 point per GHS 1 spent)
- Earn bonus points for specific actions:
  - Writing a review: 50 points
  - Referring a friend: 500 points
  - Completing your profile: 100 points
  - First purchase: 200 bonus points
  - Purchasing from a new vendor: 100 bonus points

### Points Tiers

| Tier | Points Required | Benefits |
|------|----------------|----------|
| Bronze | 0 - 999 | Standard member |
| Silver | 1,000 - 4,999 | 5% bonus points on purchases |
| Gold | 5,000 - 19,999 | 10% bonus points, free express shipping |
| Platinum | 20,000+ | 15% bonus points, free express shipping, priority support |

### Redeeming Points

- 100 points = GHS 1 discount
- Redeem at checkout by applying your points
- Minimum redemption: 500 points (GHS 5)
- Maximum redemption per order: 50% of order total
- Points expire 12 months after earning

### Viewing Your Points

- Go to **Dashboard → Loyalty Points**
- See total points balance
- View points history (earned, redeemed, expired)
- See your current tier and progress to next tier

## 10. Referral Program

### How It Works

1. Go to **Dashboard → Referrals**
2. Copy your unique referral link or code
3. Share it with friends and family
4. When they sign up and make their first purchase:
   - They receive GHS 10 off their first order
   - You receive 500 loyalty points (GHS 5 value)
   - Your referral is marked as "completed"

### Referral Rules

- Referred users must be new to SmartMart Ghana
- The referral bonus is awarded after the referred user's first purchase is delivered
- Maximum 50 referrals per year
- Self-referrals are not allowed
- Referral rewards may change based on promotions

### Tracking Referrals

- View all your referrals at **Dashboard → Referrals**
- See status: pending (signed up), completed (first purchase), rewarded (points awarded)
- See total rewards earned
- Share your referral link via WhatsApp, Facebook, or email

## 13. Security

### Account Security

- **Strong Password**: Use at least 8 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable 2FA for extra security (Settings → Security)
- **Email Verification**: Keep your email verified for account recovery
- **Regular Updates**: Update your password periodically

### Shopping Safety

- **Verified Vendors**: Only buy from verified vendors (look for the badge)
- **Secure Payments**: All payments are processed through secure gateways
- **Platform Chat**: Keep all communication on the platform
- **Report Issues**: Report suspicious listings or behavior to support

### Data Privacy

- Your personal data is encrypted and stored securely
- We never share your data with third parties without consent
- You can download your data from **Settings → Privacy**
- You can request account deletion at any time
- Payment information is never stored on our servers

### Fraud Prevention

- Never share your password with anyone
- SmartMart Ghana staff will never ask for your password
- Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true
- Report phishing emails to support@smartmartghana.com
- Always check the URL before entering login credentials
- Use the platform's payment system — never pay vendors directly

### Getting Help

- **Support Email**: support@smartmartghana.com
- **Support Phone**: +233 30 000 0000
- **Live Chat**: Chat with support from your dashboard
- **Help Center**: Browse FAQs and guides
- **Support Tickets**: Open a ticket for complex issues
