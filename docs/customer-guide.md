## Getting Started

SmartMart is your one-stop online marketplace for electronics, fashion, home goods, beauty products, and more. Shop from multiple trusted vendors, enjoy secure payments, fast delivery, and earn rewards on every purchase.

## Account Creation

### How to Register

1. Go to [smartmart.com/register](https://smartmart.com/register).
2. Enter your:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, with uppercase and numbers recommended)
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account.

### Why Create an Account?

- Faster checkout with saved addresses
- Order history and tracking
- Wishlist and saved items
- Loyalty points and rewards
- Referral program participation
- Live chat with vendors
- Product reviews and ratings

### Email Verification

After registering, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link to verify your email address. If you don't see the email:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure you entered the correct email
- Request a new verification email from the login page

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:
1. Go to [smartmart.com/forgot-password](https://smartmart.com/forgot-password).
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your email for a reset link.
4. Click the link and set a new password.

## Cart & Checkout

### Adding to Cart

1. On any product page, select quantity (if applicable).
2. Click **Add to Cart**.
3. A notification confirms the item was added.
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout.

### Managing Your Cart

1. Click the cart icon in the top navigation.
2. In the cart page, you can:
   - Change quantities
   - Remove items
   - See the subtotal, shipping, and total
   - Apply discount codes

### Checkout Process

1. **Review Items**: Confirm the items in your cart.
2. **Shipping Address**: Select a saved address or enter a new one.
3. **Shipping Method**: Choose from available options (standard, express, local pickup).
4. **Payment**: Select your preferred payment method:
   - Paystack (cards, mobile money)
   - Stripe (international cards)
   - Flutterwave (cards, bank transfer)
   - Hubtel (mobile money — Ghana)
   - Cash on Delivery (if enabled)
5. **Review & Place Order**: Review all details and click **Place Order**.

### Payment Security

- All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
- We never store your card details.
- Payment is confirmed via webhook from the payment gateway.
- You'll receive an order confirmation email once payment is verified.

### Order Confirmation

After placing your order:
1. You'll see an order confirmation page with your order number.
2. A confirmation email is sent to your registered email.
3. The vendor is notified to process your order.
4. You can track your order from your dashboard.

## Order Tracking

### Viewing Your Orders

1. Sign in and go to **My Account → Orders**.
2. See all your orders with current status.

### Order Statuses

| Status | What it Means |
|---|---|
| Pending | Order placed, awaiting payment confirmation |
| Confirmed | Payment confirmed, vendor is preparing your order |
| Processing | Vendor is packing your order |
| Shipped | Order dispatched with a tracking number |
| Delivered | Order has been delivered to your address |
| Cancelled | Order was cancelled |

### Tracking Shipments

Once your order is shipped:
1. Go to **My Account → Orders** and find your order.
2. Click **Track Order** to see the tracking number and carrier.
3. Use the carrier's website to track delivery in real-time.
4. You'll receive an email notification when your order ships and when it's delivered.

### Delivery Times

- **Standard Shipping**: 2-5 business days
- **Express Shipping**: 1-2 business days (if available)
- **Local Pickup**: Ready within 24 hours
- **International Shipping**: 7-14 business days (if enabled)

### Modifying or Cancelling Orders

- Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
- Go to **My Account → Orders**, find the order, and click **Cancel**.
- Contact the vendor via live chat for urgent changes.
- Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled but can be returned after delivery.

## Reviews & Ratings

### Leaving a Review

1. After your order is delivered, go to **My Account → Orders**.
2. Find the delivered order and click **Leave a Review**.
3. Rate the product (1-5 stars) and write your review.
4. Click **Submit**.

### Review Guidelines

- Be honest and specific about your experience.
- Focus on the product quality and vendor service.
- Don't use offensive language.
- Don't share personal information.
- Reviews help other shoppers make informed decisions.

### Vendor Ratings

- Each vendor has an overall rating based on customer reviews.
- Consider vendor ratings when choosing products.
- Leave reviews to help build trust in the marketplace.

## Loyalty Program

### Earning Points

You earn loyalty points on every purchase:

| Action | Points |
|---|---|
| Every GHS 1 spent | 1 point |
| Product review | 10 points |
| Account verification | 50 points |
| Birthday bonus | 100 points |

### Redeeming Points

- **100 points** = GHS 1 discount
- Apply points at checkout to reduce your order total.
- Go to **My Account → Loyalty Points** to view your balance.

### Point Expiry

- Points expire 12 months after they are earned.
- Check your points balance regularly to avoid losing them.

### Loyalty Tiers

| Tier | Points Required | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| Bronze | 0-499 | Standard earning rate |
| Silver | 500-1,999 | 1.25x points multiplier |
| Gold | 2,000-4,999 | 1.5x points multiplier + free shipping |
| Platinum | 5,000+ | 2x points + free shipping + priority support |

## Referrals

### How Referrals Work

1. Go to **My Account → Referrals**.
2. Copy your unique referral code or link.
3. Share it with friends and family.
4. When they make their first purchase, you both earn a reward.

### Referral Rewards

- **Referrer**: GHS 10 credit on your account
- **Referred Friend**: GHS 10 discount on their first order

### Tracking Referrals

- View your referral history in **My Account → Referrals**.
- See who you've referred and their status (pending, completed, rewarded).
- Share your code via social media, email, or messaging apps.

## Live Chat

### Chatting with Vendors

1. On any product page, click **Ask Seller a Question**.
2. Or go to **My Account → Messages** to start or continue a conversation.
3. Type your message and send.
4. You'll receive notifications when the vendor replies.

### When to Use Live Chat

- Ask about product details or specifications
- Inquire about stock availability
- Request custom orders or bulk pricing
- Ask about shipping times and methods
- Resolve order issues

### Chat Etiquette

- Be respectful and clear in your messages.
- Provide order numbers when asking about specific orders.
- Allow vendors time to respond (usually within 24 hours).
- Don't share sensitive information like passwords or card details.

## Security

### Protecting Your Account

- Use a strong, unique password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase and numbers).
- Never share your password with anyone.
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) if available.
- Log out from shared or public computers.
- Be cautious of phishing emails — SmartMart will never ask for your password.

### Recognizing Phishing

- Check the sender's email address.
- Don't click suspicious links — go directly to smartmart.com.
- SmartMart emails come from **smartmart304@gmail.com**.
- If in doubt, contact us directly using the details below.

### Safe Shopping Tips

- Only pay through SmartMart's checkout — never pay vendors directly.
- Check vendor ratings and product reviews before purchasing.
- Keep your shipping address up to date.
- Review your order confirmation email for accuracy.
- Report suspicious activity to our support team immediately.

### Data Privacy

- Your personal information is protected and never sold to third parties.
- Payment details are processed securely and never stored on our servers.
- You can request a copy of your data or account deletion at any time.
- Review our Privacy Policy for full details.
